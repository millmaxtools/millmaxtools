About Us

Founded in 1989, Mill Max Tools Pvt. Ltd. has been involved in the tool industry with manufacturing of HSS tools and supply of raw material to other tool manufacturers. In 2012, Mill Max Tools Pvt Ltd stepped into field of Tungsten Carbide to open up the first tungsten carbide rod extrusion plant in India. Today, Mill Max Tools comprises of 3 divisions:

  • Tungsten Carbide Cobalt Powder Production
  • Tungsten Carbide Rod Production
  • Import, Distribution and Retail of stocklot/surplus/new/used(refurbished) tools of High Speed Steel and Tungsten Carbide: Mill Max Machine

The company strives to cater to all cutting tool manufacturers and users in the Indian market and aims to substitute import. Our goal has always been to provide an uninterrupted supply of high-quality raw materials to our clients. We have achieved the highest stature in professionalism, quality control & integrity. We, over the years, have built strong business relationships with our esteemed clients and have a reliable team of professionals to serve you.
